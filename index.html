PRODUCT CATALOG

INDUSTRIAL NUTS

High-strength industrial nuts designed for secure fastening in heavy machinery and construction applications. Available in various sizes and materials for optimal performance.

THROTTLING CONTROL

Precision-engineered hydraulic valves for accurate flow control in industrial systems. Ensures efficient throttling and reliable operation under high pressure.

SOlENOID VALVE

Electrically operated solenoid valves for automated control of fluid flow. Compact design with fast response time for various industrial automation needs.

STAINLESS STEEL PLATES

Corrosion-resistant stainless steel plates ideal for fabrication and construction. Available in multiple grades and thicknesses for diverse applications.

STAINLESS STEEL SHAFTS

Durable stainless steel shafts for rotational components in machinery. Precision-machined for smooth operation and long-lasting performance..

PISTON RING

High-performance piston rings to seal combustion chambers in engines. Engineered for minimal wear and optimal compression efficiency.
